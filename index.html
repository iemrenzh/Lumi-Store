Our Story

Built by a girl
who got it.

LUMI started as a small pop-up in a Bondi garage and grew into the destination for young Australian women who want fashion that's actually fun — pieces that feel like them.

We source sustainable fabrics, work with small independent designers, and curate every drop with one question in mind: would we wear it out on a Saturday night?

Conscious Materials Natural & recycled fibres
Recycled Packaging Zero plastic promise
Size Inclusive XS — 3XL always in stock
